## What is Rainz Weather?

Rainz Weather is a hyperlocal weather forecasting application designed to provide highly accurate and reliable weather information for any location worldwide. Unlike traditional weather apps that rely on a single data source, Rainz combines data from multiple leading meteorological models to deliver precise weather predictions.

### Data Sources Used

Rainz aggregates weather data from:
- ECMWF (European Centre for Medium-Range Weather Forecasts)
- GFS (Global Forecast System)
- Met.no (Norwegian Meteorological Institute)
- WeatherAPI.com
- Tomorrow.io
- Open-Meteo
- Other trusted global weather sources

### AI Enhancement

All weather data is processed through AI algorithms (using Groq's Llama models) to:
- Analyze multiple model outputs for consensus predictions
- Incorporate historical weather patterns
- Provide personalized insights
- Reduce prediction errors through ensemble averaging
